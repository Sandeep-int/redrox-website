CAPABILITIES

Architected for Longevity.

Interlocking Dry-Stack System

Accurate alignment with minimal plastering. Reduces dependency on skilled labor and leads to greater efficiency.

Built-in Electrical & Plumbing

Features built-in channels meaning absolutely no wall cutting or rework is required.

FiIntegrated Crack Resistance

Shredded cloth fibers act as micro-reinforcement within the 110-ton compressed matrix, absorbing tensile stress and providing elite-level crack resistance for the lifetime of the building.

Sound Insulation

Actively absorbs sound, leading to reduced noise, echo, and vibration noise.

Thermal Insulation

Traps air to reduce heat transfer, delivering thermal comfort.

Earthquake Resistance

Ensures durability and reliability in various construction applications, especially in seismic zones.
